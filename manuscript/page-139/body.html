(She tosses her head defiantly; and he rises, ill-humoredly, adding more coolly "I've often thought that if Raina were out of the way, and you just a little less of a fool and Sergius just a little more of one, you might come to be one of my grandest customers, instead of only being my wife and costing me money.")
